Course Alignment

The syllabi of this lab aligns to the following universities in India.

  1. P. Chakorborty and A. Das. Principles of transportation engineering. PHI learning (formely prentice hall of India) 2003
  2. Khanna and Justo, “Highway Engineering”, Nem Chand & Bros, Roorkee, 1991
  3. Yoder and Witczak, “Principles of Pavement Design”- John Wiley and sons Inc(second edition) 1975.
  4. David Croney, Paul Croney, “Design & Performance of Road Pavements”- Mc Graw hill Book Co.
  5. C Venkatramaiah, “ Transportation Engineering”, Vol ume II: Railways, Airports, Docks and Harbours, Bridges and Tunnels, Universities Press.
  6. IS 73, revised 2006, IS 2720, IS 2386, IS 1201 to 1220, IS 8887- 1995, IS 217- 1986, “Indian Roads Congress”.
